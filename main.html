Alternative Recuperation System

Idea of creation

Car produces a lot of kinetic energy by moving, especially if it is a track or a bus. We can use such energy which lost in certain situats and reuse it. This is helped by the flywheel, which can absorb it.

"Every descent from the slope,
every press the brakes is
charging for the battery.
Why I need to stop due
to lack of energy if
it can be reused"
